Overview

Why take this course?
You're ready to move beyond basic conversation to discuss more complex topics.

What will you be able to do?
In this course, you will work on improving fluency and comprehension by discussing a variety of interesting topics as they relate to places around the world. For example, you’ll discuss city life in Singapore, happiness in Sweden and Norway, and technology within the context of Brazilian startups. As you discuss these topics with your tutor, you’ll cover essential vocabulary, listening, and pronunciation skills to prepare you for longer, more in-depth conversations in English.

Experience Level
Intermediate

Course Length
9 lessons

Pre-requisites
You should feel comfortable holding a conversation in English before beginning this course.
