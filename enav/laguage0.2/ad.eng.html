English Advance

Overview

Why take this course?
This course will challenge you to discuss and debate complex topics in order to increase your comfort with the English language.

What will you be able to do?
You will work on gaining fluency by speaking at length about a wide array of topics such as fad diets, exotic pets, and nature vs. nurture. You will be given opportunities to ask and answer questions and express your opinion. This course covers vocabulary at the CEFR B2-C2 levels.

Experience Level
Advanced

Course Length
10 lessons

Pre-requisites
You should be able to speak at length about abstract topics before beginning this course.
